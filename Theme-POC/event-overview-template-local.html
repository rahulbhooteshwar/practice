Connect, collaborate and share best practices

We are living at a pivotal moment in history. Economic power is shifting dramatically from the West to new economies. New markets and new leaders are exercising unprecedented influence over the course of economic change on the global scale. While complex challenges persist, new opportunities are presenting themselves each day. It will take a new community of leaders thinking, innovating and working together to create the thriving, inclusive global economy of the future.

Participants in the forum span the globe—East to West—fully embracing its inclusive vision. From the burgeoning nations of the Asia-Pacific region to the bustling metropolises of Africa and South America, representatives bring with them a range of valuable, essential perspectives.

Former U.S. Secretary of Treasury Hank Paulson, Alibaba Group Chairman Jack Ma and Former Chair of the U.S. Federal Reserve Janet Yellen are just a few of the leaders who have accepted this unique opportunity to collaborate with their peers on devising solutions to the issues facing the global economy in the year ahead.
